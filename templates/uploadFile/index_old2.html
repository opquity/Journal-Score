Introduction -- Problem Statement

We are clearly in the cognitive era. Now more than ever information is the building block of our society. It is what eat, drink, sleep and breadth. This era demands that information is immutable and if it is not, then the modern-day existential threat which our cognitive foundation is based is not on objective information but on opinions, misinformation and distortions.

Our Solution Scientific Method

"A method of procedure that has characterized natural science since the 17th century, consisting in systematic observation, measurement, and experiment, and the formulation, testing, and modification of hypothesis." - The Oxford English Dictionary

Scientific rigor is the strict application of the scientific method to ensure unbiased and well-controlled experimental design, methodology, analysis, interpretation and reporting of results leads to insightful information.

Our job is not to state we agree or disagree with any content on the web. Our job is to state if it adheres to scientific rigor.

    We have three ratings:

  • The content adheres to acceptable scientific methodology which can be disaproved
  • The content adheres to some acceptable scientific methodology, but is missing some components
  • The article is an opinion

About Us

Who are we--we are you. This is a community-based solution to a community-based problem. A core group of people realized the dangerous trend and we formed Verifiable Objective Fact-Based Information Consortium (VOFBIC) with the following goals:

  • Objective evaluation of web content
  • Encourage intelligent discourse
  • Community driven
